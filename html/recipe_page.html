Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful South Asian rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender chicken, spices, herbs, and yogurt. It is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • Biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken with onions, yogurt, and biryani spices.
  3. Boil the rice until it is about 70% cooked.
  4. Layer the rice over the chicken and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint before serving.
